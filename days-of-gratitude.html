Your birthday can be someone else's special day as well. Make your birthday memorable by giving someone the “Gift of Life”.
Donate blood. Donate for a cause.

About the Initiative

"Rakt daan sirf mahaan daan hi nahi, Jeevan daan hai"

The day we were born brought a smile to lots of faces, so why not continue the tradition of bringing smiles on our birthday. Let us make up our minds and let one of the new year's resolutions for 2021 be donating blood on our birthday and our loved ones' birthday, potentially saving up to three lives.

You can donate a pint of blood or help by collaborating with us in organizing blood donation camps in your locality, College, office, etc. You can also sponsor the blood donation camps by donating funds. The vision is to make India Blood sufficient, and we would be thankful for any source of help, be it as grants, sponsored camps, or blood units.

2020 has been a roller coaster ride, and one of the most awful things it has done other than COVID-19 itself is an 80% drop in the number of units collected via blood donation drives.

Let us all unite, donate blood at least twice a year, and distribute the gift of life.

*LetSmile celebrated their 1st bday With BloodConnect by Conducting a Blood Donation camp

How Can You Help Us?

  • You could help us by collaborating with us in organizing blood donation camps in your office, locality, etc
  • By helping us monetarily. You can also donate funds to us for organising these camps and carrying out our initiative.
  • By getting in touch with us and donating blood to your nearest bloodbank.
