Support us

Is BloodConnect a registered blood bank?

No, BloodConnect is not a blood bank. We just help keep the government recognized blood banks stocked with blood. We do not collect the blood and keep it with us. We enable these blood banks to have sufficient blood via our camps, helpline. Think of us as the layer connecting donors with blood banks in a more streamlined manner.

Can I reach the BloodConnect office and help out?

While we appreciate the gesture here, honestly we do not have an office. Our volunteers work from their respective homes / offices and we generally conduct our meetings in the common areas in an office / college. Our corporate, college partners help us with these for free and we can do all our regular catch ups there. The registered address just has a store of all our material and our documents. :) You should reach out via this form, we can certainly take forward then.

How many employees does BloodConnect have?

Zero. We are completely volunteer driven and have no full time employees

Where can you find FAQs about blood donation?

You will certainly find some of the FAQs on the page: Join Us→ Register As a Donor. We have tried to cover the concerns there. For more, please do check the Resources tab

Does BloodConnect work on anything else outside blood donation?

Yes, we do work on additional fields related to blood donation: Thalassemia, plasma requirements, etc. Our core focus remains blood donation though. We believe that is a big problem to solve

Which all cities is BloodConnect present?

We are present in various cities across India. The cities mentioned in the form, precisely cover the exact number. However we do help in other cities via our support team who can guide people on call and over mails. Our actual volunteer base though is limited by the city options in the form.
