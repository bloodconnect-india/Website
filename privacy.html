What is BloodConnect ?

BloodConnect Foundation (referred as ‘BloodConnect’) is an organization which functions with the aim to promote voluntary blood donation and spread awareness among the people to donate blood which is helpful for the poor and needy.

Contacting Blood Donor

The users (' Requesters ') shall raise a request on the website. The Helpline team of BloodConnect shall call the requester and take necessary details required for arranging blood. BloodConnect team shall try to arrange for donors or the required blood group. However, BloodConnect does not take any sort of guarantee of assurance of providing donors.

BloodConnect volunteers are experts in the field of arranging donors and shall give in full efforts to handle the request effectively.

BloodConnect shall not be held liable for injury, loss of life, or any other way in case it is unable to arrange for donors.

The details of the donors will be shared with the requester once the BloodConnect Volunteer is able to arrange for a donor. BloodConnect shall not be held liable in case a donor refuses to donate blood at any stage.
