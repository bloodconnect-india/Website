Donor Feedback Form

Thank You for coming forward and donating blood. Please spend a few minutes and tell us about your experiences related to blood donation.

Through your Feedbacks, we are constantly working to improve the experience for you and thousands of other donors that donate in our camps or at blood banks. You can also write to us at - contact@bloodconnect.org

If you donated blood in a camp, please mention name/venue of the organization where camp was done. If it was in blood bank/hospital, please mention name of the hospital
How would you rate the overall experience with BloodConnect? 5 - Excellent , 1 - Poor
Were you properly briefed about the eligibility for blood donation? Information like - When did you eat? Medication, medical history, last time donated, etc
Was the basic medial check-up done? Please select all options as applicable.
Were you served refreshment by the blood bank?
How Friendly/Helpful was the blood bank staff? 1 - None at all, 5 - Extremely
If No, then why not?
